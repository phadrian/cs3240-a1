Hi I'm Adrian, and I'm currently a final year student at the National University of Singapore, majoring in Computer Science with a focus in the fields of Software Engineering and Algorithms & Theory.

Here's a short rundown of my educational history:

I came from a catholic primary school St.Michael's School, which has since been renamed to St.Joseph's Institution Junior, given its affiliation to the secondary school. Following the trend of students, I also entered St.Joseph's Institution after completion of my PSLE.

In SJI, I decided to pursue a double Humanities course instead of the Triple Sciences course, with my focus on Geography and an elective in Literature. After 'O' Levels, I applied to St. Andrew's Junior College, with no affiliation to SJI, in order to join the H2 Computing programme there.

In SAJC, I entered as a student in the Science stream, managing to join the H2 Computing programme. In accordance with the Humanities requirement, I took an elective in Economics, alongside Math and Physics. After 'A' Levels, I decided to carry on with Computer Science, and was offered a spot in NUS Computer Science (which then had an all B's cutoff).
